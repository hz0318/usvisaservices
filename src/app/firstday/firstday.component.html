During the stay in our hotel.

Day 1 - pick up

I pick you up in Brownsville, and bring you back to Best Western Plaza hotel in Matamoros, Mexico 7 days a week.

Day 2 - fingerprint and photo

At 8.15 am, I drive you to the ASC office for fingerprinting and taking photo, wait for you outside the consulate, then drive you back to the hotel.

Day 3 - interview and go home

At 7.30 am, I drive you for your 8 am U.S. Consulate Interview appointment. Once approved, I also drive you for picking up your visa stamp and passport. Finally, I give you ride to the U.S-Mexico border.

Short tour in town

We also provide a short tour in town by car, including grocery store and super market, which makes your visa-renew as a tour in Mexico.
